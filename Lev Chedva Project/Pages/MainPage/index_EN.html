About Us

In 2020, the COVID-19 epidemic broke out throughout the world, in Israel as well. Many families who contracted the new virus collapsed mentally and as a result turned to receive assistance in a variety of areas, including: health, bureaucracy, and more. As a result of the many requests, we established "Covid-19 Emergency Center" which took care of all the needs of the families in cooperation with all the official bodies in Israel, including: the Home Front Command, the Municipality of Jerusalem, health clinics, and more. During the third wave, the need for oxygen generators arose, and later with the high demand and the lack of medical equipment, we decided to establish Lev Chedva. We try to respond 24/6, and now two years later, we were privileged to help hundreds of families, and every month we are privileged to help over 100 families with various medical equipment and absorption solutions. Over 100 volunteers take part in "Lev Chedva" who assist in the operation and transportation of the medical equipment. They are divided into several units including: transportation, jeeps, call centers, and more.
